# Arch gen: Mushroom/disc megastructure tower on central stem (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a sci-fi mushroom/disc megastructure tower: a broad flattened disc (or toroidal) upper mass carried on a slender central stem/core, with underside lighting rings, radial tension cables to the ground, and a large numeric ID band on the disc rim. Key params: disc radius/thickness and rim taper, stem height/radius, underside ring count, cable count/anchor spread, ID-band digit count, secondary satellite-disc toggle.
