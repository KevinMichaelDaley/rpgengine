# Arch gen: mall atrium balcony balustrade (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a two-level dead-mall atrium edge: an upper-floor balcony slab with a continuous balustrade (glass panel or railing) overlooking a lower court, with a recessed downlight soffit and a central tile planter box. Parameters: balcony run length, slab thickness, balustrade type/height, soffit downlight spacing, planter dimensions, and decay/staining hooks. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588896.jpg.
