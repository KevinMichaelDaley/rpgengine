# Arch gen: cantilevered folded-plate concrete stair (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a cantilevered folded-plate concrete stair: dogleg flights of open treads springing from a central spine or side wall with no outer stringer, half-landings, and a thin slab soffit. Parameters: story count, flight rise/run, landing size, slab thickness, cantilever depth, and an optional missing-rail/crumbling-edge decay flag for abandoned interiors. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931589100.jpg and pinterest_657103401931589145.jpg.
