# Arch gen: Butterfly/shed roof with bold projecting fascia (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a single-slope shed / shallow butterfly roof capped with a bold, deep projecting fascia band that reads as the building's crown, overhanging all four sides. Key params: slope pitch and direction (shed vs butterfly valley), fascia depth/thickness and color-band inset, eave overhang per side, roof deck thickness.
