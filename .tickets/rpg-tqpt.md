# Arch gen: Covered market arcade with corrugated gable skylight (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a covered market arcade: two facing rows of narrow stall bays flanking a central aisle, roofed with a low corrugated-metal / translucent-polycarbonate gable skylight on light trusses, with hanging blade signs over each stall. Key params: aisle length/width, bay count and stall width, gable ridge height and roof pitch, corrugation pitch, skylight-panel transparency ratio, purlin/truss spacing, sign-hang frequency. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588822.jpg (Kota Kembang market).
