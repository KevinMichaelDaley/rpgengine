# Arch gen: round columns with banded capitals colonnade (bpy/bmesh)


## Description

Generates a colonnade of round columns with banded capitals: cylindrical column shafts on square plinths, each topped by a stepped/banded capital collar and a projecting neck ring, arrayed in a row to carry a soffit beam or entablature band. Parameters: column count and spacing, shaft diameter/height and taper, plinth size, capital band count and flare, neck-ring depth, entablature depth, marble/stucco finish and staining. Inspired by the mall atrium columns in pinterest_657103401931588912.jpg.
