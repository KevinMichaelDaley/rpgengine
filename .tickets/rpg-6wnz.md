# Arch gen: vertical projecting blade sign (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a tall vertical projecting blade sign that mounts perpendicular to a building facade and runs several storeys: a boxed channel-letter spine reading top-to-bottom, edged with rows of chase/marquee bulbs and a bracketed wall mount. Key params: blade height/width/depth, letter count, bulb-row density, bracket arm depth, top crown/finial option, single vs double-sided.
