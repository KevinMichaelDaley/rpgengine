# Arch gen: Covered sidewalk arcade colonnade with benches (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a covered sidewalk arcade colonnade: a repeating row of posts/columns carrying a flat canopy soffit over a tiled walkway, with hanging fluorescent fixtures and periodic benches between posts. Key params: colonnade length, post spacing and section, canopy height/depth, soffit fixture frequency, bench frequency/size, paver tile grid, post-lean/damage jitter. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931589169.jpg (night sidewalk arcade).
