# Arch gen: Googie pilotis-block on stilts over carport (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a mid-century-modern (Googie) apartment mass: a solid rectangular upper storey cantilevered forward and carried on a row of thin round pilotis/stilts, leaving an open ground-level carport beneath. Key params: bay count and stilt spacing, cantilever overhang depth, stilt radius/count, upper-box footprint and storey height, ground clearance, carport bay divisions.
