# Arch gen: flat-roof brick industrial low-rise block (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a flat-roof brick industrial low-rise: a rectangular masonry box with a corbelled parapet, regularly punched multi-pane windows, a rooftop populated with vents/hatches/small penthouses, and a graffiti-ready lower wall band. Parameters: footprint, story count, window rhythm, parapet corbel profile, rooftop-clutter density, and brick-weathering intensity. Tiles into industrial-district blocks.
