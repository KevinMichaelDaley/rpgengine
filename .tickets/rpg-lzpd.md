# Arch gen: sawtooth steel-truss skylight roof (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a sawtooth (north-light) industrial roof: repeated asymmetric bays with one steep glazed face and one shallow solid face, carried on lattice steel trusses with purlins and tie rods. Parameters: bay count and pitch, glazing-face angle, truss depth, panel divisions, span, and a broken-pane decay flag.
