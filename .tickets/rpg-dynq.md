# Arch gen: pegboard retail display wall (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a pegboard/slatwall retail display wall: a wall lined with perforated pegboard or horizontal slat panels fitted with a parametric array of hooks, brackets, and shelf ledges for hung merchandise, framed by a base cabinet/counter run. Key params: wall width/height, board type (pegboard hole pitch vs slat pitch), hook/shelf mix and density, panel seam spacing, base-cabinet height. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931589213.jpg (Ammu-Nation gun-shop wall).
