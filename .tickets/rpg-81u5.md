# Arch gen: subway platform steel column row (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a subway/rail platform column row and platform edge: a raised platform slab with tactile edge, a repeating line of steel I-beam (or encased) columns down the platform carrying riveted cross-beams and a low ceiling, alongside the track bed with rails and ties. Key params: platform length/width/height, column spacing and profile (I-beam vs encased), beam depth, ceiling height, track count, rivet/grime seed. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588762.jpg (abandoned subway platform).
