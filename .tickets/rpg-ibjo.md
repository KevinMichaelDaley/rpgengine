# Arch gen: rooftop wooden water tank on steel support frame (bpy/bmesh)


## Description

Generates a rooftop wooden water tank: a tapered stave-built cylindrical tank with iron hoop bands and a conical cap, seated on a braced steel or timber support frame with cross-bracing and a ladder. Parameters: tank diameter/height, stave count, hoop band count/spacing, cap slope, support-leg height and count, bracing pattern, wood weathering, filler-pipe presence. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588884.jpg (NYC rooftop water tower skyline).
