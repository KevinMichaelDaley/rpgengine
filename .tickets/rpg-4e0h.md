# Arch gen: industrial pipe gantry and catwalk (bpy/bmesh)

Generates an industrial interior armature: a steel catwalk/stair platform threading among vertical pipe runs, valve manifolds, and draped cable/hose bundles, with pipe-rail guards. Parameters: platform level count, catwalk width, pipe-bundle count, drape sag, valve/manifold density, stair rise/run, and rust level.
