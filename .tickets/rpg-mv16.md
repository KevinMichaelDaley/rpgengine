# Arch gen: exterior steel switchback stair (bpy/bmesh)


## Description

Generates an exterior steel switchback stair rising up a building exterior: half-flights of treaded steel steps connected by half-landings that reverse direction, carried on stringers and posts against the wall, with tubular or bar railings; a curved-flight variant sweeps around a landing. Parameters: total rise / floor count, flight run and step count, landing depth, railing type and height, stringer profile, post spacing, straight vs curved sweep, paint color. Inspired by the red switchback/curved stair in pinterest_657103401931589078.jpg.
