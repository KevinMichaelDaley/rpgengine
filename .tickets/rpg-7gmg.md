# Arch gen: Concrete industrial warehouse column hall with mezzanine (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a large concrete industrial warehouse interior hall: a grid of heavy square/round columns supporting deep beams, with a raised mezzanine ledge/gallery along one or more walls and openings for stairs. Key params: hall extents, column grid spacing, column section and beam depth, storey clear height, mezzanine height/depth and edge-parapet, ledge opening count, surface spall/stain variation. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931589151.jpg (warehouse party hall).
