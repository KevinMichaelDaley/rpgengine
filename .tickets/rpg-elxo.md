# Arch gen: decayed strip-mall parapet storefront block (bpy/bmesh)

## Description

Generates a low, long commercial retail block: a flat-roofed masonry/tilt-up box with a continuous raised parapet band (with occasional raised sign pylons), a repeating rhythm of storefront bays, and boarded or broken glazing. Parameters: bay count and width, wall height, parapet band height and pylon spacing, storefront glazing ratio, and a boarded-up/decay ratio. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588881.jpg and pinterest_657103401931588882.jpg.
