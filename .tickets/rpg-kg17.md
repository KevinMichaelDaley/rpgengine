# Arch gen: cast-iron ornamental streetlamp (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a cast-iron ornamental street lamp: a fluted tapered base pedestal, a tall shaft, and one or two curved scroll arms terminating in acorn/teardrop luminaire heads. Key params: pole height, base flare/flute count, arm count (1-2) and reach/curvature, luminaire style, finial presence. Emits mounting bosses for street-sign or banner attachment. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931572029.jpg (foreground LA lamp posts).
