# Arch gen: masonry arched doorway with keystone (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a masonry entrance framed by a semicircular arch with voussoirs, a projecting keystone, and springer imposts over recessed double doors with a fanlight. Parameters: opening width/height, arch rise, voussoir count, keystone projection, impost band depth, fanlight subdivision, and door-leaf count. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588993.jpg.
