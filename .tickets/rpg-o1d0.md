# Arch gen: exterior steel stoop staircase (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a straight exterior stoop stair rising from sidewalk to a raised entrance: open steel treads on stringers, a top platform, and pipe or bar railings on both sides. Parameters: step count, rise/run, stair width, stringer depth, rail height/style, platform depth, and open-riser vs closed. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588993.jpg.
