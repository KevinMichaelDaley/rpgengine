# Arch gen: shipping-container stack wall (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a wall/yard formed from stacked ISO shipping containers, with corrugated side panels, corner castings, end doors with locking bars, and stagger/gaps between stacks. Parameters: columns and rows (stack width/height), container length mix (20/40 ft), corrugation pitch, stagger jitter, door-face orientation, and rust/dent weathering.
