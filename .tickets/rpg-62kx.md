# Arch gen: glass atrium dome skylight (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a glazed atrium dome skylight: a segmented radial/geodesic glass-and-mullion dome (hemispherical or shallow cap) sitting on a circular drum ring, with radial rib mullions, ring purlins, and optional hanging light coffers below. Key params: dome radius, rise/height ratio, radial segment count, ring divisions, mullion depth, drum-wall height, glazing panel gap. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588890.jpg (dead-mall dome atrium).
