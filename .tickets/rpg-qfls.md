# Arch gen: exposed timber floor-joist ceiling (bpy/bmesh)

Generates an unfinished basement ceiling of parallel timber floor joists with a perpendicular rim/beam, subfloor planking above, and clamped pipe/cable runs threading between joists. Parameters: joist spacing, joist depth/width, span length, beam count, plank gap, and sticker/graffiti-decal density hooks.
