# Arch gen: backlit box fascia sign band with channel lettering (bpy/bmesh)


## Description

Generates a horizontal illuminated fascia sign band that spans a shopfront: a shallow boxed light-cabinet with a translucent face panel, extruded channel lettering / phone-number text mounted proud, and a mounting bracket to the wall above the storefront. Parameters: band length/height/depth, letter extrusion depth and kerning, face-panel inset, emissive intensity, mounting standoff, optional under-canopy soffit. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931589114.jpg and the shop sign in pinterest_657103401931589078.jpg.
