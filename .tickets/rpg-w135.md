# Arch gen: trapezoidal concrete drainage canal (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a trapezoidal concrete flood/drainage canal running as a spline: sloped side revetment walls, a narrow low-flow channel at the invert, top-edge safety railings, and periodic weep holes and expansion-joint scoring. Parameters: channel length/curvature, top width, invert width, wall slope angle, wall height, and rail on/off per side. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588714.jpg and pinterest_657103401931588856.jpg.
