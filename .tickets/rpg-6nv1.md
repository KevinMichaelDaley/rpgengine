# Arch gen: hillside public stair street (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a long public staircase climbing a steep hillside street between flanking buildings, with intermediate landings, side handrails/parapets, and a shallow drainage gutter. Parameters: total rise, step rise/run, flight length before landing, stair width, rail style, and terrain slope adaptation. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588788.png.
