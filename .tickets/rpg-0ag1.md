# Arch gen: rolling metal shutter shopfront (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a rolling metal shutter shopfront: a corrugated/slatted roll-down door in a recessed masonry opening, with a top coil housing, side guide channels, a bottom lock rail, and pull handles. Parameters: opening width/height, slat pitch, open fraction (0=closed to 1=rolled up), housing size, and grime/graffiti UV region.
