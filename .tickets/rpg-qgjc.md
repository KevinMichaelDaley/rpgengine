# Arch gen: overhead bridge/gantry crane (bpy/bmesh)


Generates an overhead travelling bridge (gantry) crane: a box-girder bridge spanning between two wall-mounted rails, an end-truck at each side, a trolley with hoist drum and hook block, and a suspended operator cab. Parameters: span, girder depth, trolley position, hoist-drop length, cab size, and load-rating decal placement. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588749.jpg.
