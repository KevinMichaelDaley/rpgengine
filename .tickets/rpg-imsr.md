# Arch gen: Projecting canvas storefront awning (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a projecting canvas/fabric storefront awning: a sloped or box-shaped fabric cover on a light frame cantilevered over the sidewalk, with a valance flap and optional support rods. Key params: awning width/projection/drop, slope angle or box depth, valance flap height and scallop count, frame-rod visibility, fabric sag amount, stripe/panel subdivisions.
