# Arch gen: steel-frame multi-pane industrial window wall (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a large steel-frame industrial window wall: a grid of small rectangular lights divided by slender mullions/muntins within a heavier structural frame, optionally with pivoting hopper vents. Parameters: overall width/height, light count in X and Y, mullion thickness, frame depth, arched-head option, and a broken/missing-pane mask for decay.
