# Arch gen: curved stone concourse stair with tubular pipe handrail banks (bpy/bmesh)


## Description

Generates a curved masonry concourse stair as found in transit stations: wide stone/terrazzo treads that fan on a curve, a low intermediate landing, and multiple parallel banks of tubular steel pipe handrails dividing the width, with a tactile-paving strip at top and bottom. Parameters: stair width and curve radius, tread count and rise/run, number of parallel handrail banks, pipe diameter and post spacing, tactile-strip width, wall-tile backdrop toggle. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588570.jpg (curved subway station stair with pipe handrails).
