# Arch gen: caged projecting balcony (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a caged projecting balcony: a shallow cantilevered slab or bracket-supported ledge enclosed by a full-height welded steel bar cage (anti-fall / anti-intrusion), optionally roofed, with a small access door/window behind. Key params: balcony width/depth, cage bar pitch and diameter, cage height, roof on/off, bracket count, corrosion seed.
