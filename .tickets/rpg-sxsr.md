# Arch gen: caged bulkhead wall lamp (bpy/bmesh)


Generates an industrial caged bulkhead wall lamp: a round or oval base plate, a glass dome/jar, and a protective steel guard cage of bent ribs, wall-mounted with a conduit stub. Parameters: dome radius, cage rib count, cage projection, base shape, mount-arm angle, and emissive-bulb intensity. Small reusable fixture for alleys and doorways.
