# Arch gen: metro station tile-clad column (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a fat structural column typical of subway/metro halls: a round or square shaft clad in ceramic tiles with a darker base band and a simple capital/head where it meets the ceiling slab. Parameters: shaft height/radius, cross-section (round vs square), tile module size, base-band height, capital flare, and grime/wear level. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588777.jpg and pinterest_657103401931588662.webp.
