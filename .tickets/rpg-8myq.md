# Arch gen: Timber utility pole with crossarms and transformers (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a timber utility pole with stacked crossarms: a tapered round pole carrying horizontal crossarms with insulator pins, mounted cylindrical transformers, and stub anchors for spanning wires to neighbors. Key params: pole height/taper, crossarm count and vertical spacing, insulators per arm, transformer count/size, guy-wire anchor toggle, wire-attach stub points, lean/weather jitter.
