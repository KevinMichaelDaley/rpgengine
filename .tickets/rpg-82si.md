# Arch gen: Art Deco stepped tower crown (bpy/bmesh)

## Description

Generates the setback crown of an Art Deco skyscraper: a series of receding stepped tiers capped by a zigzag/chevron parapet, with vertical fluted piers rising through the setbacks and stylized fin buttresses. Exposes tier count, per-tier setback depth and height, pier width/spacing, chevron amplitude, and an optional crest ornament or letter-sign mount. Glazed-terracotta panelization is driven by a spandrel/mullion grid parameter. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931571890.jpg (Eastern Columbia tower).
