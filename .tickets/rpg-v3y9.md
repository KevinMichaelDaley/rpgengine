# Arch gen: cantilevered corrugated shop awning canopy (bpy/bmesh)


## Description

Generates a cantilevered shop awning / canopy projecting over a storefront or stall: a sloped or flat frame of angle-iron arms with a corrugated-metal or sagging fabric cover, a drip edge, and wall mounting brackets; supports both a rigid corrugated variant and a slumped tarp variant. Parameters: awning width/projection/slope, cover type (corrugated vs fabric), corrugation pitch, sag amount, bracket count, front valance drop, rust/staining. Inspired by the roast-meat stall canopy in pinterest_657103401931588841.jpg.
