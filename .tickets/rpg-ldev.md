# Arch gen: paired escalator bank (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a bank of parallel escalators (and/or a flanking fixed stair) climbing between two levels, each with an inclined truss, stepped tread band, side balustrades with moving-handrail caps, and top/bottom landing plates. Parameters: unit count, rise height, incline angle, tread width, balustrade height, adjacent-stair toggle, and landing depth.
