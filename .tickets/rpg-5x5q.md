# Arch gen: commercial strip storefront row (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a row of single-story, flat-roof commercial shops sharing a party-wall grid, each bay topped by a tall parapet fascia that doubles as a signboard band (e.g. "DRUGS", "MARKET") above a boxed transom and a full-width storefront opening. Parameters: bay count and per-bay width, storefront height, parapet/fascia height, transom band depth, per-bay setback/projection jitter, and material/color per bay. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931571902.jpg.
