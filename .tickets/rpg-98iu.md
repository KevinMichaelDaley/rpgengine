# Arch gen: brick service wall with roll-up door and louver vents (bpy/bmesh)


## Description

Generates a utilitarian masonry service wall / building rear elevation: a brick or block wall with a roll-up sectional overhead door, steel-frame man-door, banks of wall louver vents, a lintel course, and surface-mounted conduit and downpipes. Parameters: wall width/height, brick coursing and color, roll-up door size and slat pitch, man-door presence, louver-vent count/size, conduit run density, lintel/sill detailing, grime. Inspired by the brick alley wall with roll-up doors and louvers in pinterest_657103401931588785.jpg.
