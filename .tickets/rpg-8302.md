# Wire real renderer + GI into the client; rename demo_client->client

Replace the client's inline forward shader with the render-world module (T6) driven by the streamed scene, so the networked client renders the level with clustered forward+ PBR, baked lightmaps, and dynamic probe GI. Rename demo_client -> client and keep its orchestrator expandable.

## Design

Hook the render-world build after asset streaming completes (demo_client Stage 7 render + the procgen-load block are the insertion points). Networked bodies (BODY_SPAWN/MESH_DATA) become dynamic renderables appended after render_scene_mark_dynamic; their transforms come from the prediction/interp pools each frame. Client already links liball.a (full renderer). Keep --record video capture. Rename target + source (update Makefile line ~1976).

## Acceptance Criteria

Client loads a scene descriptor, streams assets by priority, and renders it with the real forward+/GI path; networked bodies appear as dynamic renderables lit by the scene GI; runs at interactive fps; binary renamed to build/client.
