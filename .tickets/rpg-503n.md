# Arch gen: Applied rectangular relief-tile facade panel grid (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a regular grid of applied rectangular relief tiles/panels raised off a stucco facade to create a rhythmic shadow-line pattern (as opposed to structural masonry). Key params: tile width/height, horizontal and vertical gap, rows/columns or fill-region driven layout, relief/standoff depth, per-tile jitter and random-omission ratio for weathering. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931571894.jpg (red tile grid on the Hauser front).
