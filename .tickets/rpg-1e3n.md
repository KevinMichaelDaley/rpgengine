# Arch gen: projecting neon script sign (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a projecting neon sign: bent glass-tube lettering (from a supplied text/curve) mounted on a backing panel or open frame, with a wall bracket, transformer box, and emissive tube material plus a subtle glass-support grid. Parameters: text/curve, tube radius, glow color, backing panel on/off, bracket arm length, and a flicker/dead-segment decay flag. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931589036.jpg and pinterest_657103401931588955.jpg.
