# Arch gen: big-box retail shell with sign panel (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a big-box retail store shell: a large low windowless masonry/EIFS box with a flat membrane roof carrying rooftop HVAC units and roof drains, a tall street-facing brick sign panel, and a long thin storefront entry canopy running along the base. Key params: footprint width/depth, wall height, sign-panel size/position, rooftop-unit count, parapet height, entry-canopy length/projection, decay (roof-pooling, paint-peel) seed.
