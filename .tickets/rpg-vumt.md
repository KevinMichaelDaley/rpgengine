# Arch gen: fuel-station canopy (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a cantilevered flat-roof fuel-station canopy: a wide slab deck carried on 1-4 stout round columns, with a deep perimeter fascia band that carries horizontal accent stripes (brand livery) and a recessed soffit with a grid of downlights over the pump islands. Key params: deck width/depth/overhang, column count and radius, fascia depth, stripe count/colors, soffit light spacing, ground clearance. Optionally emits pump-island bases. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931571996.jpg (desert Mojave-red canopy).
