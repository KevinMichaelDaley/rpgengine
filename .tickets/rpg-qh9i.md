# Arch gen: glass elevator tower shaft (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a freestanding glass elevator tower shaft: a tall rectangular clad column rising through an atrium void, faced with tile/panel on the structural sides and glazing on the cab-visible side, topped by a machine-room box and threaded by rail guides. Key params: shaft height, footprint width/depth, storey stops, cladding panel size, glazed-face fraction, machine box size, door-opening placement. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588890.jpg (pink mall elevator column).
