# Arch gen: hanging-goods market stall with heat lamps under canopy (bpy/bmesh)


## Description

Generates a hanging-goods market/street-food stall: a counter/display base under a low canopy, a horizontal hanging rail with S-hooks carrying suspended goods, caged heat/warming lamps, and a signboard fascia above. Parameters: stall width/depth, counter height, canopy drop and slope, hanging-rail length and hook count, heat-lamp count and cage style, signboard size, clutter/grime amount. Inspired by the roast-meat vendor stall in pinterest_657103401931588841.jpg.
