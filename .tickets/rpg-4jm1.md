See ref/scene_editor_design.md §5.5. Animation mode with two sub-modes: Rig (bone placement, constraint editing, no timeline) and Animate (timeline visible, keyframing active). Tab toggles between them. :anim rig / :anim animate commands.

Animation mode activates with sub-mode toggle. Rig and Animate sub-modes work. Cross-reference with ref/scene_editor_spec.md and ref/scene_editor_ux.md for requirements and interaction details.
