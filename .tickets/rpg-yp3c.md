# Arch gen: brutalist stepped-parapet storefront entry (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a low brutalist commercial building front with a crenellated/stepped parapet crown, blank precast wall panels, and a deeply recessed entry framed by a cantilevered flat canopy over a full-height glazed vestibule flanked by low retaining side walls with ramps/steps. Parameters: facade width, parapet step count/height, canopy depth, vestibule glazing divisions, and side-wall length. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588883.png.
