# Arch gen: wall-mounted billboard panel (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a large wall-mounted billboard/ad panel flush or slightly proud of a blank building side wall: a rectangular sign face on a shallow batten sub-frame with perimeter trim and optional top flood-light gantry. Key params: panel width/height, wall stand-off depth, frame border width, floodlight arm count, mounting-bracket grid. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931572041.jpg (iPhone / building-side billboards).
