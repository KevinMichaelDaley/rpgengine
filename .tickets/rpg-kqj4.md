# Arch gen: welded steel cage balcony enclosure (bpy/bmesh)

## Description

Produces a welded steel security 'cage' enclosing a balcony or window: a projecting box frame of angle iron clad in mesh or closely spaced bars, with a sloped or flat top and a bolted wall flange. Parameters: cage width/height/projection, bar vs mesh infill, bar spacing, top slope angle, and drying-rack rail toggle.
