# Arch gen: suspended alley sign cluster (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a cluster of suspended/projecting shop signs over a narrow alley: multiple backlit light-boxes and hanging placards cantilevered from facades on brackets and hung from overhead wires at varied heights and angles. Key params: sign count, per-sign size range, projection depth, vertical stagger, bracket vs wire-hung ratio, tilt jitter. Meant to fill an alley volume densely. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588613.jpg (Korean alley) and pinterest_657103401931588744.jpg (Varanasi banner).
