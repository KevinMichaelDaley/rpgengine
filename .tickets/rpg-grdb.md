# Arch gen: Atomic-age starburst wall ornament with address plaque (bpy/bmesh)


Generates an atomic-age Sputnik/starburst applied wall ornament: a central disc with radiating tapered spikes of varied length, plus an optional address-number plaque and thin trailing wire whiskers with ball tips. Key params: spike count and length distribution, central disc radius, spike taper, whisker count/curl, plaque text-plate size, standoff depth from wall. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931571894.jpg (red starburst with '1436').
