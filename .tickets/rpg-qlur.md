# Arch gen: flat tar rooftop with parapet and mechanical penthouses (bpy/bmesh)


## Description

Generates a flat built-up (tar) rooftop as an assembly: the roof deck with a raised perimeter parapet and coping, plus scattered mechanical penthouses, bulkhead stair housings, vent stacks, ductwork, and satellite/antenna clutter. Parameters: roof footprint, parapet height and coping profile, penthouse count/size, vent-stack density, HVAC unit placement, graffiti/weathering intensity, drainage scupper spacing. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588884.jpg (graffitied tar rooftops).
