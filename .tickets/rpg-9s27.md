# Arch gen: Beaux-Arts modillion cornice + rusticated facade (bpy/bmesh)

## Description

Produces a heavy classical crowning cornice (bed mould, dentil band, modillion/console brackets, projecting corona and cyma cap) sitting atop a rusticated ashlar masonry wall with banded joints. Parameters: cornice projection and height, dentil count/pitch, modillion spacing, number of rusticated courses, joint reveal depth, and window-head keystone toggle. Inspired by the ornamented masonry facade in the foreground of pinterest_657103401931571890.jpg.
