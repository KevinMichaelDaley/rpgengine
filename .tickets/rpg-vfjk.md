# Arch gen: roll-up shutter storefront (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a roll-up (corrugated) security shutter storefront: a shopfront opening framed by a lintel and jambs, filled by a rolled steel slat curtain that can be parameterized open/partly-open/closed, with bottom rail, guide channels, and a top barrel housing. Key params: opening width/height, slat pitch and corrugation depth, open fraction, guide-rail width, housing box size, weathering/dent seed. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588606.jpg and pinterest_657103401931588888.jpg (market shutters).
