# Arch gen: projecting enclosed balcony sunroom box (bpy/bmesh)


Generates a projecting enclosed balcony / sunroom box cantilevered off a facade: a shallow glazed volume with a framed window grid on the front and returns, a solid spandrel/soffit underside, and support brackets or a slab lip. Parameters: projection depth, width, height, glazing divisions per face, spandrel height, and bracket style. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931588800.jpg and pinterest_657103401931572033.jpg.
