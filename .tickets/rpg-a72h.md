# Arch gen: boarded-up plywood storefront bay (bpy/bmesh)

Generates a decayed, sealed storefront bay where the original glazing is covered by plywood/panel sheets set into a recessed frame above a low plinth, with weathered seams, warping, and stain streaks. Parameters: bay width/height, panel subdivision (sheet count), plinth height, recess depth, board warp amount, and weathering/decay intensity. Inspired by pinterest_657103401931571921.jpg.
